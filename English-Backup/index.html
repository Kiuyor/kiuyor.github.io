巧记英语

001 define —— 明确“定义”

define definition identify identifiable identity determine determinism judge

002 behavior —— 注意自己的每个“行为”和“举止”

behavior behavioral conduct manner performance

003 demand —— 为自己的“主张”发声

demand require claim acclaim claimant command assert assertion request affirm allegation

004 ignore —— 请选择“忽视”

ignore ignorant neglect overlook

005 own —— 此物被我“占有”

own ownership property monopoly possession possess occupy occupation

006 state —— 我来“说明”这个理论

state statement statesman understatement illustrate specific specify detail explanation explanatory interpretation interpret misinterpretation exemplify outline

007 view —— 各种“看”

view observe observation witness glance glimpse

008 suggest —— 给你我的“建议”

suggest suggestion refer reference mention advocate propose proposal urge lobby recommend recommendation convincing convince persuade persuasive advisory

009 announce —— 公开“宣布”

announce announcement publish publication publicity publicize release advertisement advertising advertiser declaration

010 deal ——“处理”好乱糟糟的局面

deal approach executive solution handle administration regulation regulator regulate regulatory arrange supervise supervision surveillance cope tackle resolve monitor

011 consider —— 用脑“思考”

consider consideration reflect conceive deduce ponder

012 admit —— 我“承认”，的确有这事

admit recognize recognition acknowledge

013 produce ——“生产”出产品

produce productivity generate generative output yield manufacture manufacturer

014 sense —— “感知”周围的状况

sense sensory perceive conscious consciousness subconscious detect detector cognitive comprehension

015 predict ——“预知”未来

predict prediction unpredictability forecast foreseeable

016 create —— “创造”出新模式

create innovation innovative formulate formulation invent novelty

017 promote ——“促进”目标的完成

promote further contribute contributory advance advanced boost motivate motion motive assist motor propel

018 criticize —— 各种“骂”法不留情

criticize criticism critic critique blame condemn

019 found —— 立地而起，“建立”起来！

found establish established establishment construct construction

020 provide ——“提供”必要的物质

provide supply cater equip render provision
